Mission

Why SUPAY RED SPACE exists

SUPAY RED SPACE is an offensive security village focused on creating a serious, community-driven space for red teaming, pentesting and exploit research, with a long-term goal of becoming a national reference in Peru.

Mission

Elevate offensive security practice

The village connects practitioners, students and researchers around curated labs, simulations and talks that reflect real attack paths, not theoretical slides.

Community

Grow a high-signal network

The space is designed to be vendor-neutral and technically demanding, encouraging collaboration, mentoring and peer review across the offensive security community.

Vision

Become a regional reference

The long-term roadmap includes exporting the village to major conferences, partnering with local and international events and maintaining a strong technical identity.

Activities

What happens in the village

Each edition of SUPAY RED SPACE will mix talks, guided workshops and open hacking time on purpose-built infrastructure covering network, cloud, application and adversary simulation scenarios.

Hands-on labs

Offensive ranges

Curated attack paths with infrastructure tailored for red team exercises, persistence, lateral movement and post-exploitation practice.

Capture the flag

Focused challenges

Short CTF-style tracks aligned with the village theme, from exploit development and web abuse to cloud and hybrid environments.

Talks & workshops

Deep technical content

Sessions prioritize practical offensive techniques, tooling and case studies, with live demos whenever possible.

Community

Mentoring & office hours

Informal spaces where experienced operators, researchers and students can discuss paths into offensive security and ongoing projects.
